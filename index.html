"Amrutmulya - Special Jaggery Kulfi"

Taste... That You Will Remember!
  • Established in 2020, Amrutmulya Jaggery Kulfi was born out of our passion to give the classic Indian dessert a healthy yet delightful twist. With deep respect for traditional flavors and a commitment to quality, we set out to craft a kulfi experience that blends the rich, natural sweetness of jaggery with the creamy goodness of kulfi.
  • Our Jaggery Kulfi is made using premium, organic jaggery, which not only enhances its unique taste but also brings out the natural goodness of this ancient sweetener. We take pride in preparing each kulfi with love, care, and the finest ingredients, ensuring it is as wholesome as it is delicious.
  • Free from artificial sweeteners and preservatives, our kulfi offers a healthier option while retaining the authentic flavors you know and love. Whether you're a long-time kulfi enthusiast or someone looking to try something new, Amrutmulya Jaggery Kulfi promises a guilt-free indulgence that beautifully blends tradition and innovation.

Why Choose Special Gulachi Kulfi?

Indulge in the authentic taste of tradition with our Special Gulachi Kulfi, crafted with pure jaggery for natural sweetness and a healthier twist on the classic kulfi.

  • 01 100% Natural Sweetener

    We use pure jaggery instead of refined sugar, providing a rich flavor while maintaining traditional goodness.

  • 02 No Artificial Preservatives

    Our kulfi is free from artificial sweeteners and preservatives, ensuring a pure and healthy dessert experience.

  • 03 Crafted with Love

    Every batch of our Special Gulachi Kulfi is handmade with care, bringing you the authentic taste of tradition in every bite.

Benefits of Special Gulachi Kulfi

Our Special Gulachi Kulfi is not only delicious but also packed with health benefits:

  • Jaggery is rich in Iron, Magnesium, Potassium, and Vitamin C, along with other essential minerals.
  • Helps regulate blood sugar levels naturally.
  • Aids in digestion, boosts immunity, and supports detoxification.
